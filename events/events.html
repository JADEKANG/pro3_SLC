DAY 2

The ‘Self-Love Workshop Series’ includes expert-led sessions, guided journaling, and a panel discussion with influencers and mental health advocates sharing insights on overcoming self-doubt. The day wraps up with ‘Show Yourself’ art therapy sessions, where participants explore self-acceptance through creative group projects led by art therapists.

DAY 3

The final day includes a 'Letters to My Past/Future Self' workshop, where attendees reflect on their growth and set intentions for continued self-compassion. The festival concludes with a live performance, with special invitations extended to Taylor Swift and BTS, whose “Love Yourself” campaign aligns perfectly with the festival’s message of self-love and acceptance.
